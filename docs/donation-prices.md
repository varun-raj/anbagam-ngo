# Anbagam NGO Donation Price List

### **General Impact Tiers**
*   **₹1,000 — Individual Care**: Covers personal care, grooming, and individual treatment support for one resident.
*   **₹1,500 — Medicines**: Funds daily medication for one resident (administered 1–3 times daily).
*   **₹3,500 — Full-day Care**: Comprehensive one-day care for a single resident including meals, medication, and support.
*   **₹21,000 — Full Day Meals**: Sponsors a full day of nutritious meals for every resident across all Anbagam centers.
